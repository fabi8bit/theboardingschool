About Us

Once upon time there was a sad city where living not so many people and there was no other option for youngster than popping beer caps and count them. But Luca arrived out of nowhere with his Mike McGill board and dropped an ollie flip in front of us. We fell in love with him so we started to stalk him until he convinced him self that he had to teach us how to skate… the rest is history… for the details please call us! We love skateboarding more than Top Gun!

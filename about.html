About Us

Once upon time there was a sad city with only a few inhabitants, where there was nothing to do for youngsters other than popping beer caps and counting them. Those youngsters were us! But Luca arrived out of nowhere with his Mike McGill board, and dropped an ollie flip in front of us. We fell in love with him, so we started to stalk him until he was convinced that he had to teach us how to skate… the rest is history… for the details please call us! We love skateboarding more than Top Gun!
